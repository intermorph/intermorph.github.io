Avant-propos

Artwork collection

All the images were generated using a custom StyleGAN2-ada implementation trained on a handcrafted dataset of 1200 nebulae and abstract pictures. The only post-processing applied was some standard color correction and a little bit of image sharpening. You can download the PNG images at full resolution here.



Music video

This audio-reactive video is the result of sequentially interpolating the latent vectors of 370 unique generated samples. The procedure is straightforward: different audio tracks (s.a. the kickdrum) are mapped to different vectors that get triggered whenever they sound, which makes the generated images smoothly react to the music. You can find more details about the implementation on my Twitter.
