Cycles

Artwork collection

All frames were generated by means of a custom StyleGAN2-ada implementation trained on a handcrafted dataset of 500 spray drawings. The post-processing merely involved adjusting the black-and-white threshold levels and flipping the images. You can download the collection at full resolution here.



Music video

This audio-reactive video is the result of sequentially interpolating the latent vectors of more than 100 unique generated samples. To maintain the simplicity and darkness of the track, the kick and clap were consistently mapped to their respective seeds throughout the entire duration of the song.
